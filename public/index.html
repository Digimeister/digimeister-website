▸ ABOUT DIGIMEISTER

Digimeister makes online games: multiplayer and social, the kind that only work because other players are in them. Founded in Fukuoka, Japan in 2022, all-in on online since 2024.

▸ WHAT WE DO

For studios shipping online, the studio handles co-dev and programming. Netcode, matchmaking, live systems. We ship our own games too, so we know what it takes.

▸ LET'S BUILD SOMETHING

Publishers, studios, developers: if online is in the brief, get in touch.
